

## Future Extension: News Dashboard Integration

To keep our ecosystem fully informed, the Vision extends to include a News Dashboard within the UI. This dashboard will:
- Crawl and aggregate industry news, security updates, and compliance requirements.
- Use robust, existing web crawlers (avoiding from-scratch implementations) to extract deep data.
- Standardize the data format into an internal schema before display.
- Surface actionable insights regarding emerging zero-days or new sustainability standards directly to developers.
